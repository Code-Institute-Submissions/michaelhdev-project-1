About the Band

50 Years of Experience

The Monkees were formed in Los Angeles in 1965 by Bob Rafelson and Bert Schneider for the American television series The Monkees which aired from 1966 to 1968. The band have continued touring the past five decades.

Peformed live at over 800 events

Over the past five decades the band has toured and performed extensively. Selling out many of the well know venues around the world. The band continue to tour and perform. They perform at large events and small events. If you would like the ledendary monkees to perform at your event book them today!

Band Members

Davy Jones

Davy hails from England and he is one of the founding member of the group. He is also its lead singer.

Micky Dolenz

The funny man of the group and one hell of a drummer. Micky was born in Los Angeles and he has been playing the drums since the age of 4.

Michael Nesmith

Michael was born in Houston Texas. He always wears a hat and he is the lead guitarist with the group

Peter Tork

Peter was born in Washington D.C. He has played the piano from a very young age. He is the keyboardist and bass guitarist of the Monkees
